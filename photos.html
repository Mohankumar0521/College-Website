Aims and Objectives

The prime aim and objective of MRCE are to provide the best library and information services to its academic and research community. With this motto, MRCE is striving to realise the following objectives.

Objectives

  • To provide an environment for blended learning
  • To provide innovative and user-oriented services by adopting state of technologies
  • To develop user-oriented policies and programmes
  • To improve library staff capabilities by training and development initiatives
  • To provide and deliver general and customised information services to the user community
  • To support and enhance teaching and learning activities by facilitating and promoting the effective use of information resources and services available through MRCE offline and online
  • Provide an information-rich environment that supports and encourages excellence in research in science & technology
  • Realising and supporting MRCE in achieving its mission and goal and as a centre of excellence in the academic and research arena

About Us

At MRCE Knowledge Resource Center, we provide various mechanisms to feed young and curious minds. We engage with our student, teaching and research communities in sharing knowledge, connecting them with the vast collections from around the world. Our mission for over four decades has been to advance the learning, research, and pursuit of knowledge.
